YOU ARE HOME

At Kibo Restaurant, we are more than just a dining venue. We are a local family restaurant where ambition and community spirit drive us. Our dedicated young team works tirelessly to provide the best food and service, aiming to grow our enterprise and further support our community. Come and feel at home with us, where every visit is a delightful adventure.

Kibo Restaurant began as a small venture driven by a group of friends from Watamu with a vision to offer an exceptional experience to tourists visiting their area. They noticed that traditional hotel services often fell short of reaching the beaches where tourists were relaxing, swimming, and enjoying themselves. Inspired by the idea of enhancing comfort in this tropical paradise, they created a unique haven for refreshment and entertainment.More than just a top-notch venue, Kibo Restaurant provides a genuine taste of local life in Africa, specifically in Watamu. Here, food is prepared and served with a warm, homely touch while maintaining a professional standard. We provide Christmas gifts for children and offer food and clothing to their families. Although our contributions may be occasional, we are committed to spreading joy and reflecting the generosity of our clients.
