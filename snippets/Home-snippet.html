WELCOME TO ECSTACY

HAKUNA MATATA!

Welcome to Kibo Restaurant: Your Seafood Sanctuary calling all seafood enthusiasts! Dive into an exciting experience at Kibo Restaurant, your destination for fresh, vibrant, and delicious seafood. Located on the stunning shores of the Indian Ocean in Fortam Beach - off Jacaranda Road, Watamu, our restaurant offers breathtaking ocean views and a menu that highlights the best of the sea.

What Makes Us Unique:

  • Fresh Ocean Flavors: We pride ourselves on sourcing the finest, freshest seafood, delivered daily and lovingly prepared by our talented chefs. Every bite captures the true essence of the ocean.
  • World-Inspired Dishes:Our diverse menu offers dishes from around the world, from classic coastal favorites to inventive fusion creations. Explore a range of flavors, including Italian-inspired specialties.
  • Captivating Atmosphere: Enjoy your meal in a captivating setting with stunning waterfront views. Our ambiance, whether you’re dining upstairs with a perfect view of the waves or in our elegantly decorated main area, elevates your dining experience
  • Dedicated to sustainability: We are dedicated to responsible sourcing and ethical practices to preserve our oceans and ensure a sustainable future for seafood. By employing local youth, we contribute to the community's well-being and growth.
  • Outstanding Service: Our enthusiastic team is devoted to making every visit memorable. Expect attentive service, expert recommendations, and warm hospitality that makes you feel at home.

    • Beyond Just Seafood:

  • Wide Selection: We cater to diverse tastes with options for vegetarians, gluten-free diners, and other dietary preferences.
  • Refreshing Beverages: Complement your meal with a selection of beer and drinks such as soda and water.
  • Exclusive Dining & Events: We host unforgettable gatherings in our beautifully decorated upstairs and downstairs dining areas or in our scenic outdoor spaces.
  • Live Cooking: Enjoy live cooking on select evenings, adding a special touch to your dining experience.
